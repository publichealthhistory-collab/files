Recommended reading

This page is under development and will be expanded over time.

This section will bring together books, articles and other resources related to the themes, case studies and historical periods explored in the Public Health History video series.

The aim is not to provide an exhaustive bibliography, but to highlight selected works that offer useful historical context, critical perspectives and accessible entry points into public health history.

Wherever possible, open-access materials will be prioritised. Some links may also direct readers to published books or other resources for further exploration.
